Generate Executive Summary of provided documents in markdown.

YAML frontmatter:
---
title: Executive Summary
description: High-level synthesis of key documents and insights
version: 1.0
date: [today]
author: Docgen
category: Analysis
status: PRODUCTION_READY
tags: [summary, synthesis, executive]
---

Sections IN ORDER:
- Overview (1 sentence: what documents you analyzed)
- Prerequisites (what the user needed to understand)
- Key Findings (3-5 major insights across all documents)
- Critical Decisions Made (what was decided where)
- Important Metrics (numbers that matter)
- Recommendations (what to do next based on docs)
- Gaps Identified (what's missing or unclear)
- Implementation Readiness (are you ready to execute?)
- Risk Assessment (what could go wrong)
- Expected Output (table: document, key finding, action)
- Troubleshooting
- Why This Matters
- What's Next

Quality rules:
- SYNTHESIZE across documents (not just summary of one)
- BE SPECIFIC about findings (with sources)
- HIGHLIGHT conflicts/disagreements if any
- IDENTIFY missing information
- MAKE recommendations actionable

Token budget: 1500 max
